Welcome to RS 13

RS 13 is a free css template released under Creative Common License by Roshan of ramblingsoul.com. This template is free to download and use in any way you want provided you keep the link back to my website in tact and unaltered.

RS13 is inspired by the wonderful interface of Mac OS and iTunes. I am just trying to imitate it through this template.

The top menu is done in Lavalamp Menu, originall created by Ganesh of Gmarwaha.com.
